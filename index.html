A Little Bit of Lou

On March 24th, 1979, the year of our Lord, I was born in Grand Forks, North Dakota on an airforce base to Carlos and Martha Rivera.
According to one website I visited it was 26.1 degrees that fateful day, snow depth was about 3 inches and the wind speed was an average or 19 mph, or so the website Weatherspark.com says. Upon exiting my mother's birth canal the doctor immediately assigned the "male gender" to me, I assume it was because at birth I had a penis, which is usually a sign that the baby is a male, or at least according to un-woke science (knuckle-draggers, SMH).

We eventually relocated to Chicago, the home of Da Beloved Bears, crooked politicians, gang violence and deep dish pizza (I think I covered all the important aspects of the city).

My mother was a deeply Christian woman, but prior to her conversion...not so much, but as a child all I remember was church, church, small groups, church, etc...
I was raised alongside two sisters and a half brother, there were some good times and there were some bad times and a lot of "eh" in between, blah, blah, blah.

Fast forward past all my preadolecent and adolecent trials and error, missteps and follies, I eventually got married and had three beautiful daughters. Eventually I would suffer a split in my household followed by a painful divorce and then I met Amber.

Amber I met on a crappy dating site, Plenty of Fish, I assume she liked me because eventually we'd get married and adopt a dog, Jarvis, and three cats/kittens, Emily, Bjorn and Freyja.

To be honest I don't know what else to write, I mean, I do, but it would be a long, boring read, so instead here are some photos of when I was a child. My mom only took like 3 photos of me because taking photos of your children is hardwork.
